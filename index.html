A Great Actor Lost...

This man needs no introduction!! Many actors will come and go but no one might be able to beat the intensity which he used to deliver. Sushant Singh Rajput was known for his charismatic, confident and gentleman personality. In his career spanning 11 films he played a variety of characters from a casual happy-go-lucky man in Kai Po Che to a dacoit in Sonchiriya
